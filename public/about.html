What is LEADPAC

Leadership & Entrepreneurship Advanced Development for Poor and Average Children (LEADPAC) Foundation.


LEADPAC Foundation a.k.a Project20-Nigeria is an indigenous Teenagers’ and Youths Leadership and Entrepreneurship Development Organization. Its operational nomenclature - Project20-Nigeria is specifically for the purpose of strategic and tactical vision design, direction and execution.


It creative idea is centered at bringing effective and true leadership development & training, entrepreneurial and empowerment skills to the poor, average, vulnerable and less privileged teens and youths in our society who cannot to a large extend pay to acquire such training and skills now and/or in the nearest future.


Our activities are targeted towards a positive and growth mindset shift of these young generation of Nigerian potential leaders between the ages of 10-35years. The vision is detailed to cover the entire country until every ordinary Nigerian child who is disadvantaged either by birth, family, economy and socio-political status is adequately equipped with relevant informed-knowledge and intellectually, emotionally and physically empowered.

This we believe shall create an exceptional and professional platform for them to compete favourably from an advantaged position with others and become very successful in their contributions to national development and socio-economic and political stability of our society.


The vision focuses on providing qualitative, competitive and a redefined corporate leadership and entrepreneurship approach to these group of young minds, therefore creating an atmosphere where they can discover themselves (self-identity & purpose), develop their gifts and potentials into the 21st century marketable skills. They shall be inspired and encouraged to undergo all the important developmental and value added capacity building for their future wellbeing and contributions to the transformation and prosperity of our society..

Our Vision

To Nurture and Fulfill the Dream of a Transformed, Realistic and Prosperous Nigeria.


Our Mision

To raise, grow, develop, train, transform, educate and empower one million (1million) poor, average and vulnerable teens and youths into potential and matured true and effective leaders in each state of the Federation with pursuit for competence, diligence, dignity, integrity, sense of confident, peace loving, trustworthiness, honesty and good self-esteem across the country who are visionary, with good moral and spiritual standard, patriotic, humble and ever ready to fight for the unity, harmony, peaceful co-existence and the common good of Nigeria and Nigerians.

HISTORY

LEADPAC Foundation a.k.a Project20-Nigeria is an Africa’s focused think-tank community of teenagers and youths who approaches leadership with a different mindset and determined to identify different leadership views. These views are then subject to critical study and analysis that leads to developing a true, effective and a more people-oriented and conscious system of leadership that address the emotional, spiritual, moral and intellectual considerations of mankind with the view to contributing positively and consistently to the development of their immediate communities in personal, domestic, organizational, regional, national, continental, global and generational human relationship issues and discussions.


The above National and Global Leadership Giant (NGLG) was conceived in July, 1993 by a 16year old Master Aidam Mark Ettan, also known and called officially as Princemark Mark Ettan from an average parents who had a very difficult financial experiences during his education pursuit both at Primary and Secondary School levels considering his family background. He will spend some of his school weeks in the farm with his parents to raise school fees before returning back to school for studies.


His leadership gifts showed up in school when he appointed a prefect in three key positions – Assistant Senior Prefect Boys, Laboratory Prefect and the Chapel Prefect. On graduation, he was awarded the best student in Sciences, one among the best Prefects and overall academic best in his set at Community Secondary School, Biakpan – Biase Local Government Area of Cross River State.


After his Youths Service (NYSC) at Ota, Ogun State in 2003 where he led as the Zonal Coordinator of Nigeria Christian Corpers Fellowship (NCCF), he got a job as a teacher with Access High Schools and later join the State Secondary Education Board in August, 2006. On the 15th of December 2012(about 19years after the vision was conceived), at Ishie Town Primary School, Calabar Municipality, Cross River State – Nigeria. The birth of the brain-child LEADPAC Foundation a.k.a. Project20-Nigeria became a reality and was witnessed by nineteen (19) Secondary School students (teenagers between the ages of 12 and 17) from Government Secondary School, State Housing Estate, Atekong Drive, Calabar – Cross River State.


The vision is targeted at creating a platform for average and disadvantaged young minds to discover themselves, develop and horn their gifts, talents and potentials, deploy all their abilities to the service of their immediate communities and the nation – Nigeria and by extension the continent of Africa. The National, Continental and Global Headquarters of LEADPAC Foundation a.k.a Project20-Nigeria (Both Administrative and Field Directorates) shall be at Calabar, Cross River State, Nigeria.
