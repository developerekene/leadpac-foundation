Welcome to Leadpac Foundation

leadership and entreprenurship development at Project 20 Nigeria

Leadpac foundation a.k.a Project-20 is an Indigenous Teenagers and Youth leadership and Entrepreneurial development organization.

Its creative idea is centered at bringing effective and true leadership development and training, entreprenurial and empowerment skills to the poor average vulnerable and less privilage teens and youths in our society who cannot to a large extent pay to aquire such trainig and skills now or in the near future.

Our Benefits
  • Rediscovering of your life purpose.
  • Building leadership capacity to lead effectively in our community
  • Developing Entrepreneurial potential, skills for economic, financial growth and empowerment.
  • Cultivating personal awareness and high self-esteem among peers.
  • Mental development, Physical fitness and wellness.

what we do

We Train

We take the task in the Academy to teach the sequences and values needed to becoming good leaders and entrepreneurs.

We Build

We show our students the step by step methods they need to gradually build themselves and get where they want to get to.

We Empower

Every teenager has a legal right to create, we capitalize on those rights and lend a helping hand by empowering them.

We Guide

In today's changing world, navigating in the right direction is paramount. We infuse in our students the ability to making the right choices to protect their future.

We Mentor

We understand clearly that in life mentorship is needed. We are counsellors and great mentors to the students.

We Reform

In a corrupt society, there is need for a configuration of the mind, we provide that service.
